Behind The Counter


At Hajvairy Meat Shop, we think good food and quality ingredients should be available to everyone at prices that don’t cost an arm and a leg. We started operating in Rawalpindi/Islamabad in 1994. Since then, we’ve seen plenty of changes in the way people approach food - and we couldn’t be more excited. Come and check us out in person and discover something delicious today.
